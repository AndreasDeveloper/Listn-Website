Kamikaze Kicking In . .

Kamikaze is the tenth studio album by American rapper Eminem. Previously unannounced, the album was released on August 31, 2018. Kamikaze was met with generally positive reviews from critics. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream publications, the album received an average score of 62, based on 16 reviews. Aggregator AnyDecentMusic gave the album 5.6 out of 10, based on their assessment of the critical consensus. Throughout the album, lyrics criticize other musicians and several have responded publicly. Eminem and rapper Machine Gun Kelly have had an ongoing feud for several years, and Kelly released a diss track in response to "Not Alike" titled "Rap Devil". On streaming music service Spotify, five tracks from Kamikaze were in the top 10 of the week of September 4, including the top two spots.

DAMN

Damn is the fourth studio album by American rapper Kendrick Lamar. It was released on April 14, 2017, by Aftermath Entertainment, Interscope Records and Top Dawg Entertainment. Damn received widespread critical acclaim and topped the charts in the United States and Canada. Damn has been characterized as conscious rap with elements of trap, R&B and pop. Damn received widespread acclaim from critics. Beast Mode 2

In January 2015, Future released the mixtape Beast Mode in collaboration with Zaytoven, which was released to critical acclaim. In an interview with The Fader, Zaytoven stated that approximately 100 songs were recorded for Beast Mode 2 between 2016 and 2018. Mosi Reeves of Rolling Stone wrote "The Atlanta rapper’s latest reminds us why he’s a master of sing-rap blues even as that style begins to move beyond his massive influence." The mixtape debuted at number three on the Billboard 200 based on 73.5 million streams of its songs, which Billboard equated to 57,000 album-equivalent units, granting the rapper his ninth top ten and the highest-charting streaming-exclusive album in the chart. In the Canada, Beast Mode 2 debuted at number 13 on the Canadian Albums Chart, marking first release to not debut in the top ten in the country.
